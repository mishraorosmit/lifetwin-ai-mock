## 🧠 What is LifeTwin?

**LifeTwin** is an AI-powered **digital twin platform** that allows users to simulate life decisions and visualize their potential outcomes.

Instead of guessing the future, users can **test their decisions through intelligent simulations**.

> Imagine seeing the long-term impact of your choices *before* making them.

---

## 🚀 Core Concept

LifeTwin creates a **virtual representation of a person (Digital Twin)** and runs simulations on lifestyle and life decisions.
